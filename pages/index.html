Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Who is eligible to apply to Telecall Institute of Technology?

To apply to Telecall Institute of Technology, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years old.

  • Possess a valid ID photocopy.

  • Provide the necessary documents, including:

    • 2 copies of the Birth Certificate
    • 2 copies of the Marriage Certificate (if married)
    • 2 copies of the Diploma
    • 2 copies of Form 138/138/TOR/ALS Certificate
    • 3 pieces of 1x1-inch photos
    • 2 pieces of 2x2-inch photos
    • 2 pieces of passport-sized photos

2. How do I apply to Telecall Institute of Technology?

To apply, follow these steps:

  • Complete the online application form on our website.

  • Prepare all required documents, including the ones mentioned in Question 1.

  • Submit your application and documents to our admissions office in person or through our designated application portal.

3. What programs does Telecall Institute of Technology offer?

Telecall Institute of Technology offers a variety of academic programs. Please visit our Programs page on our website for a detailed list of available courses and programs.

4. What is the application deadline?

Application deadlines may vary depending on the program and semester. Please refer to our Admissions page on the website or contact our admissions office for specific application deadlines.

5. How long does the application review process take?

The application review process typically takes several weeks. You will be notified of your application status via email or mail once the review is complete.
